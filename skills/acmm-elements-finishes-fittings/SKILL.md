# ACMM Vol 2 — Finishes & Fittings (12–16)

This skill covers elements 12–16. For Substructure/Superstructure see `acmm-elements-substructure-superstructure`. For services see `acmm-elements-services`.

**General note re Finishes:** where a finish incorporates special formwork, only the cost **extra over** rough formwork is in the finish. Rough formwork is part of structure. Whole cost of special formwork in the one sub-element only where finish is in structure (e.g. external wall finish) or where concept of finish doesn't apply (e.g. smooth forms to slab soffit concealed by false ceiling).

---

## 12 WF — Wall Finishes

**Definition:** Finish + decorate all internal faces of Columns, External Walls, Internal Walls.

**INCLUDES:** finishes to internal faces of external walls/columns; acoustic wall linings; extra costs for face bricks, face/coloured blocks, off-form concrete; splashbacks, dados, regulation wall vents.

**EXCLUDES:** finishes to Internal Screens (NS); skirtings (FF); cornices (CF); external finishes to external walls/columns (EW); finishes to both sides of external screens (EW); all protective non-decorative coatings.

**Unit:** m² — WF = EW + 2 × NW (less glazed/self-finished areas in curtain or screen wall sub-elements).

## 13 FF — Floor Finishes

**Definition:** Finish to upper floors and substructure for walking on.

**INCLUDES:** all preparatory work and finishing; balcony floor finishes; skirtings; screeds; timber floor finishes; dividing strips; mats and matwells; duct and pit covers; carpeting as permanent floor finish; timber/other finishes to concrete floors; finishes to steps in one floor level.

**EXCLUDES:** structural screeds and toppings (SB, UF); landing and stair finishes (SC); trafficable roof covering (RF); timber framed floors (UF, SB); finishes to external thresholds (ED); door sills (ED, ND).

**Unit:** m² — element includes all floor finishes to areas measured in GFA. FF = sum of FFCA to FFMT + any in FFNE.

## 14 CF — Ceiling Finishes

**Definition:** Finish + decorate all internal soffits of upper floors and roof over rooms, and external soffits over unenclosed covered areas.

**INCLUDES:** preparatory work; suspended false ceilings; proprietary suspended ceiling systems; acoustic ceiling linings; extra cost for off-form concrete; linings to roof lights; ceiling manholes; framing to bulkheads and cornices.

**EXCLUDES:** eaves soffits (RF); stair and landing soffits (SC); ceiling joists where not suspended (UF, RF); soffits of projecting overhangs (EW); all protective non-decorative coatings (UF, SC, RF); air conditioning grilles (AC).

**Unit:** m² — CF = net area of ceilings measured in plane of ceiling (excl. returns to sides of beams). All sub-elements include cornices.

### Sub-elements (m² each)

| Code | Sub-element | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| CFFF | Paintwork on Factory-Finished Surfaces | Precast, steelwork etc.; extra cost of formed finishes; includes paintwork |
| CFFC | Fair Face to Concrete incl. Paintwork etc | |
| CFRF | Render and/or In-situ Plaster | |
| CFPL | Fibrous Plaster and Plasterboard | Includes paintwork |
| CFSL | Sheet Linings | Includes paintwork and trim; excl. CFPL |
| CFTB | Timber Boarding | Includes polishing and trim |
| CFBF | Battened Finish | Includes polishing and trim |
| CFAC | Tile and Sprayed Acoustic Finishes | Includes decoration and trim |
| CFFP | False Ceilings — Proprietary Type Suspension System | Suspended false ceilings, proprietary suspension, linings, decoration |
| CFFN | False Ceilings — Non-Proprietary Type Suspension Systems | Non-proprietary suspended framework, linings, decoration |
| CFRL | Roof Light Ceiling Finishes | Includes cheeks to dormers, lining to roof light wells, paint and trim |
| CFMG | Ceiling Manholes | — |
| CFNE | Not Elsewhere | % |

CFFF–CFAC are applied finishes to framework/surfaces in elements UF and RF. CFFP and CFFN each comprise complete suspension, framework and finish.

## 16 SE — Special Equipment

**Definition:** Items of equipment of unitary, commercially available type and/or of a type not covered by other elements.

**INCLUDES:** window cleaning, gymnasium, mortuary and photographic equipment; audio-visual aids; laboratory, laundry, kitchen and central sterile services department (CSSD) equipment; dental and workshop equipment; boiling water units; sink heaters; laboratory stills; special blackout facilities; bed pan washers; linen and refuse disposal equipment; refrigerators and refrigerated drinking water coolers; incinerators; sanitary macerators; circulating fans; cold/hot/gas/other valves and cocks, controls, electric wiring and piping integral with this equipment; specified Builders Work in connection.

## When to apply this skill

When: filling the Finishes or Fittings sections of a cost plan; allocating finish or fitting items in a BOQ to elemental codes; checking what's INCLUDED vs EXCLUDED in WF/FF/CF/FT/SE; identifying "extra over" cost relationships (e.g. NWBK structure → WF for facings); deciding whether an equipment item belongs to FT (built-in fitments) or SE (commercially available equipment) or YY (loose furniture); confirming the unit of measurement for a sub-element.
